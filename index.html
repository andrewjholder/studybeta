How it works 🎯

From zero to exam-ready in four simple steps.

1

Create a Deck

Name your deck, pick a subject, icon and color. Organize by class, chapter, or topic.

2

Add Your Cards

Add Q&A, True/False, Multiple Choice, or Fill-in-the-blank cards. Group them by topic.

3

Start a Session

Start studying and the Pomodoro timer kicks in. Choose to shuffle, study by group, or review missed cards only.

4

Track Progress

See your score, accuracy %, and streak. Know exactly which cards to revisit.
