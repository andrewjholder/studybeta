1 Who we are

My Study Buddy is a free, browser-based flashcard and study app designed for students, teachers, and homeschooling families. Our goal is to make self-study fun, accessible, and effective for learners of all ages — from kindergarten through adulthood.

We are a small independent project currently in beta. The app is hosted at andrewjholder.github.io/studybeta and is offered completely free of charge with no subscription, no account required, and no advertisements.

📚 My Study Buddy is a passion project built to help people learn — not a data company. Your trust means everything to us.
2 What information we collect

We collect as little information as possible. Here is exactly what we do and do not collect:

What we may collect:

• Feedback emails — If you choose to email us at hello@mystudybuddy.app, we receive your email address and message. This is entirely voluntary.

• Basic analytics (future) — We may add anonymous page-visit analytics in a future version. If we do, we will update this policy and use only privacy-respecting tools.
What we do NOT collect:

• Your name, age, or personal profile
• Your flash card content, decks, or study data (stored only on your device)
• Location data  •  Payment information  •  Any information from children without parental consent
3 How we use your information

If you send us feedback by email, we use your email address for one purpose only: to reply to you. We may use the content of your feedback anonymously to improve the app — but never attributed to you without permission.

We will never use your email to send unsolicited marketing, add you to mailing lists without asking, or share it with any third party.

💬 If you email us, we email you back. That’s the whole relationship.
4 How your data is stored

All of your study data — decks, flash cards, session history, and settings — is stored exclusively in your browser’s localStorage. This means:

✓ Your data never leaves your device
✓ We have no access to your decks or flash cards
✓ Your data is not uploaded to any server
✓ You own your data completely

Because localStorage is tied to one browser on one device, we provide CSV export so you can always back up your own data. A future opt-in cloud sync feature may be offered — always with full transparency and a clear choice.

5 We do not sell your data
🔒 We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third party for commercial purposes. We never have. We never will.

My Study Buddy has no advertising partners, no data brokers, and no investors requiring us to monetize user information. If this ever changes — which we do not anticipate — we will notify all users clearly and in advance.

6 Children’s privacy

My Study Buddy is designed to be safe for children. Our free math, color, and sight word decks are specifically created for K–3 learners.

✓ We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
✓ No account or registration is required to use the app
✓ No study data is transmitted from the device
✓ The app contains no advertising or in-app purchases
✓ Parents and teachers can use and supervise the app safely

If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us at hello@mystudybuddy.app and we will delete it promptly.

7 Cookies & browser storage

My Study Buddy does not use tracking cookies. We use browser localStorage — a standard web technology — to save your decks and settings locally. This is not a cookie and is not accessible to us or any third party.

GitHub Pages (our host) may set technical cookies as part of normal web hosting. We do not control these and do not use them to identify or track you.

You can clear your data at any time via your browser settings or the “Clear all my data” button in app Settings.

8 Third-party services
🔗 GitHub Pages — Hosts our website. May log basic server data (IP, browser type). See GitHub’s Privacy Statement.

🔗 Google Fonts — Fonts loaded from Google servers. See Google’s Privacy Policy.

🔗 jsPDF — Runs entirely in your browser to generate PDF exports. No data sent anywhere.

We do not use advertising networks, social media trackers, or any analytics that profile individual users.

9 Your rights & choices
✓ Delete your local data — Use “Clear all my data” in Settings or clear localStorage in your browser

✓ Export your data — Export any deck as CSV or PDF at any time

✓ Request deletion of your email — Email us and we will delete any personal information you’ve sent

✓ Opt out of future communications — Simply don’t email us, or ask us to remove you

Contact us at hello@mystudybuddy.app for any privacy requests.
